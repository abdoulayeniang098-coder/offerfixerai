1. What we collect

When you use Offer Fixer AI, we may collect the following information:

  • Account data: email address, name, and password (hashed) when you register
  • Offer content: the text you submit for analysis
  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, analysis frequency
  • Payment data: processed securely by Stripe — we never store card numbers
  • Device data: browser type, operating system, IP address (anonymized)

We never sell your personal data to third parties. Your offer content is yours.

2. How we use your data

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide and improve the Offer Fixer AI service
  • Process your AI analysis requests
  • Send transactional emails (account confirmation, billing receipts)
  • Improve the accuracy and quality of AI-generated results
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

We do not use your offer content to train AI models without your explicit consent.

3. AI processing & your offer content

Offer Fixer AI uses the Anthropic Claude API to analyze your offer. When you submit content for analysis:

  • Your offer text is sent to Anthropic's servers for processing
  • Anthropic's own privacy policy applies to this processing
  • We do not store your raw offer content beyond the current session unless you explicitly save an analysis
  • Saved analyses are encrypted at rest and accessible only to your account

Tip: Avoid including sensitive personal data (passwords, financial credentials, private client info) in your offer submissions.

4. Data storage & retention

Your data is stored on secure servers in the European Union (GDPR-compliant). Retention periods:

  • Account data: retained while your account is active + 90 days after deletion
  • Saved analyses: retained until you delete them or close your account
  • Session offer content: deleted immediately after the analysis is returned
  • Payment records: retained for 7 years as required by law

5. Third-party services

We use the following trusted third-party services:

  • Anthropic Claude API — AI analysis engine
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Vercel — hosting infrastructure
  • Plausible Analytics — privacy-friendly, cookieless analytics (no personal data)

Each provider is bound by their own privacy policy and GDPR-compliant data processing agreements.

6. Your rights (GDPR & CCPA)

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to fix inaccurate information
  • Deletion — request complete account and data deletion
  • Portability — export your saved analyses in JSON format
  • Objection — opt out of any marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below. We respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies

We use minimal, functional cookies only:

  • Session cookie: keeps you logged in (expires when you close the browser)
  • Preference cookie: remembers your plan and language settings

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or fingerprinting. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work correctly.
